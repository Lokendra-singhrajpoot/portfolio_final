Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, Â¼ cup maida (all-purpose flour), a pinch of baking soda, and 2 tbsp ghee. Add milk gradually and knead a soft, smooth dough. Rest for 10 mins.
  2. Make small, crack-free balls from the dough. Keep them covered to prevent drying
  3. Heat ghee/oil on medium-low flame, and fry the balls until golden brown, stirring gently for even color.
  4. In parallel, make syrup by boiling 1.5 cups sugar + 1.5 cups water + cardamom + rose water. Add fried jamuns into warm syrup and soak for 2+ hours.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Blend dry cashews into a fine powder (don't over-blend or it'll release oil).
  2. In a pan, boil sugar + water until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  3. Add cashew powder to syrup and stir on low flame till it forms a dough-like consistency (non-sticky and leaves the pan).
  4. Cool slightly, knead with a bit of ghee, then roll the dough thin and cut into diamond shapes. Garnish if desired.
